CHINA, Tientsin mint, pattern "Dragon and Phoenix" dollar, Year 12 (1923), small characters variety, PCGS UNC details / graffiti.民国12年龙凤壹圆，PCGS UNC details / graffiti。L&M-81; Kann-680; KM-Y336; WS-0114; Chang-CH241; Wenchao-885. Among the most popular and beautiful of classic Chinese numismatics is this "dragon and phoenix" pattern silver dollar, this example with a few unfortunate scratches in the field to the right of the dragon yet otherwise possesses the sharp design details and plentiful mint luster as expected for an Uncirculated piece. L&M-81; Kann-680; KM-Y336; WS-0114; Chang-CH241; Wenchao-885.  这枚“龙凤”图案银币是中国经典钱币中最受欢迎且精美的品咱之一。尽管该枚币龙右侧的币面有几道遗憾的划痕，但整体细节清晰，光泽充足，符合未流通币的标准。PCGS：49673258
