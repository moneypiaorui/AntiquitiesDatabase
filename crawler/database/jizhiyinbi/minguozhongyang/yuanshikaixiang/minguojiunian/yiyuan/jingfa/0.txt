CHINA, Republic, dollar, Yuan Shih-kai, Year 9 (1920), ex-Peterson.民国9年袁世凯像壹圆,  ex-Peterson。L&M-77; Kann-666; KM-Y329.6; WS-0181-2. 26.85 grams. Only the second coin and the first of this type that weve offered from this shipwreck. Rare provenance to a World War II Japanese transport ship sunk on December 30th, 1942 in Simpson Harbour off Rabaul during a bombing run by B-17s of the USAAF 43rd Bomb Group. AU with polished surfaces from post-salvage cleaning, no noticeable corrosion. Pedigreed to the collection of the late Dr. Mendel Peterson (Curator of Underwater Archeology for the Smithsonian Institution), with original Brandenberg tag and information.
