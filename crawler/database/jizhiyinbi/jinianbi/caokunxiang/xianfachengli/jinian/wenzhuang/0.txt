Struck to commemorate the Proclamation of the Constitution and featuring the bust of Tsao-Kun in civilian attire, a well-struck example with crisp design elements throughout, the obverse fields heavily hairlined from prior cleaning, now toning over with golden and purple hues that get richer around the rims.
