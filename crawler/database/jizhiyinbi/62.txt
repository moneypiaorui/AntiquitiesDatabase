1900年代水手长的哨子钱币银手鍊首饰。 CHINA. Boatswains Whistle Coin Chain Jewelry, ND (ca. 1900s). 此串钱币由六枚中国广东三分六釐和六枚维多利亚时代香港伍分交替串联而成（KM-Y-199 和 KM-5）。每枚钱币皆焊接了两个小环，再以链环从两侧穿过小环将钱币固定在腰带上。链条顶端是一个带有交叉影线设计的银质水手长哨子，它穿过最上方一枚香港伍分钱币上的小孔固定。 A string of twelve coins alternating between the Chinese Kwangtung 3.6 Candareens (KM-Y-199) and Victorian Hong Kong 5 Cents (KM-5). The coins are connected in a belt via chain links down both sides, fastened through the two loops that are soldered to each individual coin. A silver boatswains whistle with a cross-hatched design tops the chain, attached through a hole in the uppermost Hong Kong 5 Cents coin.
